# LM358
Dual low-power op-amp that works from a single supply.
## Pin Map
1 OUT A, 2 IN- A, 3 IN+ A, 4 GND/V-, 5 IN+ B, 6 IN- B, 7 OUT B, 8 V+.
## Absolute Maximums
Supply up to 32 V single-supply; inputs should stay within common-mode range.
## Typical Use
Sensor buffers, low-frequency filters, comparators in beginner labs.
## Common Faults
Expecting rail-to-rail output, input common-mode too close to V+, missing ground.
