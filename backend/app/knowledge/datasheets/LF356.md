# LF356
JFET-input single op-amp used in filters, integrators, and high-impedance lab front ends.

## Pin Map
DIP-8: 1 offset/null, 2 inverting input, 3 non-inverting input, 4 V-, 5 offset/null, 6 output, 7 V+, 8 no connect or compensation variant dependent.

## Absolute Maximums
Keep supply and input voltages within the selected vendor limits. In student labs, do not exceed typical +/-15 V rails, avoid input beyond rails, and do not short the output.

## Typical Use
High-impedance buffers, active filters, integrators, sample-and-hold style demonstrations, and sensor preamplifiers where low input bias current matters.

## Common Faults
Missing negative rail, pinout confused with dual op-amp packages, floating non-inverting input, output saturation from no DC return path, and unstable breadboard wiring with long input leads.
