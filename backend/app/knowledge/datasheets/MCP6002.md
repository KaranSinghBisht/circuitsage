# MCP6002
Dual rail-to-rail CMOS op-amp for low-voltage circuits.
## Pin Map
1 OUT A, 2 IN- A, 3 IN+ A, 4 VSS, 5 IN+ B, 6 IN- B, 7 OUT B, 8 VDD.
## Absolute Maximums
Supply around 6 V max; inputs must not exceed rails by diode drop.
## Typical Use
Battery sensor buffers and Arduino analog front ends.
## Common Faults
Powered from 5 V but driven above VDD, capacitive load oscillation, wrong package pinout.
