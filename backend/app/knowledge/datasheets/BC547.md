# BC547
NPN small-signal transistor common in amplifier labs.
## Pin Map
Typical TO-92 flat-face order is C-B-E, but confirm vendor package.
## Absolute Maximums
Small collector current and power; keep base-emitter reverse voltage low.
## Typical Use
Common-emitter gain stages and small switching.
## Common Faults
Pin order confused with 2N3904, collector bias too low, no emitter degeneration.
