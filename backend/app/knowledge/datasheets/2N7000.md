# 2N7000
Small-signal N-channel MOSFET used for low-current switching and logic-level demonstrations.

## Pin Map
TO-92 pinout is commonly drain, gate, source when viewed from the flat face, but vendor variants exist. Confirm the datasheet for the exact part.

## Absolute Maximums
Respect drain-source voltage, gate-source voltage, and package power dissipation. Keep Vgs within rating and avoid inductive spikes without a clamp.

## Typical Use
Low-current LED switching, simple level shifting, small relay-driver demonstrations only when current is within rating, and MOSFET gate-drive teaching.

## Common Faults
Source not tied to ground in low-side switching, gate left floating, gate driven below the required Vgs, drain/source swapped, and no flyback diode on an inductive load.
