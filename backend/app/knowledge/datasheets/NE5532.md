# NE5532
Dual low-noise audio op-amp.
## Pin Map
1 OUT A, 2 IN- A, 3 IN+ A, 4 V-, 5 IN+ B, 6 IN- B, 7 OUT B, 8 V+.
## Absolute Maximums
Supply typically up to +/-18 V; respect input differential and output current limits.
## Typical Use
Audio gain stages and active filters.
## Common Faults
Single-supply bias missing, load too heavy, rails reversed.
