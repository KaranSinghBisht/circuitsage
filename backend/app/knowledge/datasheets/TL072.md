# TL072
Dual JFET-input op-amp for audio and lab filters.
## Pin Map
1 OUT A, 2 IN- A, 3 IN+ A, 4 V-, 5 IN+ B, 6 IN- B, 7 OUT B, 8 V+.
## Absolute Maximums
Supply up to about +/-18 V; input within rails; avoid output shorts.
## Typical Use
Active filters, buffers, integrators, audio preamps.
## Common Faults
Missing negative rail, floating non-inverting input, pinout swapped with single op-amp.
