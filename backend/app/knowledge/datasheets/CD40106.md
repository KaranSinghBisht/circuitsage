# CD40106
Hex Schmitt-trigger inverter.
## Pin Map
Six inverter gates; VDD and VSS power pins vary by package drawing.
## Absolute Maximums
CMOS inputs must stay within rails; observe supply max for 4000 series.
## Typical Use
Debouncing, oscillators, waveform cleanup.
## Common Faults
Floating CMOS input, no decoupling capacitor, supply pins reversed.
