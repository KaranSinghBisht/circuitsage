# BS170
Small N-channel MOSFET for low-current switching.
## Pin Map
TO-92 pinout is commonly D-G-S but vendor variants exist.
## Absolute Maximums
Low current and power; Vgs usually +/-20 V.
## Typical Use
Logic-level small-signal switching and level shifting.
## Common Faults
Drain/source swapped, gate left floating, load current too high.
