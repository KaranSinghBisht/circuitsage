# 7805
Fixed 5 V linear regulator.
## Pin Map
TO-220 front view commonly IN-GND-OUT.
## Absolute Maximums
Input voltage and power dissipation limited by heat; needs dropout headroom.
## Typical Use
5 V logic rails from higher DC input.
## Common Faults
Input/output pins swapped, no decoupling capacitors, overheating from excessive voltage drop.
