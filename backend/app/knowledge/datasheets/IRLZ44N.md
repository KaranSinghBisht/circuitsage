# IRLZ44N
Logic-level N-channel power MOSFET.
## Pin Map
TO-220 front view usually G-D-S.
## Absolute Maximums
Respect Vds, Id, Vgs +/-20 V, and thermal limits.
## Typical Use
Low-side switching for motors, lamps, and LED strips.
## Common Faults
Gate floating, no common ground with controller, missing flyback diode on inductive load.
